Tacos

Ground Beef Tacos is one of our favorite weeknight family meals, especially on Taco Tuesdays!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Red onions
  • Sour Cream
  • Taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in skillet over medium high heat.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Serve warm over tortillas with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese and red onions, or your other desired toppings.
